# Comparison of Tesla Model Y and Ford Mustang Mach-E for Sales Fleet

## Tesla Model Y
- **Charging**: Fast charging support (up to 250 kW), adding up to 144 miles in 15 minutes at Supercharger stations.
- **Performance**: 0-60 mph in 3.3 seconds (Performance variant), total output of 460 hp, max torque of 741 Nm.
- **Range**: Up to 357 miles (RWD), 303 miles (Performance variant)
- **Charging Time**: 144 miles of range in 15 min with Supercharging; varies from 50 hours (110/120V) to 9 hours (220/240V)
- **Cargo Space**: 74.8 cubic feet (seats down), 30.2 cubic feet (seats up)
- **Notable Features**: Efficient range, ample cargo space, strong charging infrastructure

## Ford Mustang Mach-E
- **Price & Variants**: Starting around $57,690, available in multiple trims, including a high-performance GT variant.
- **Performance**: GT Performance version with 480 hp, 0-60 mph in 3.3 seconds, and advanced technologies such as customizable drive modes.
- **Range**: Up to 320 miles (standard version), with some configurations reaching up to 480 miles
- **Charging Time**: Approximately 7.7 hours (Standard Range, Level 2); 32 minutes (DC Fast Charge from 10%-80%)
- **Performance**: 0-60 mph in ~5.8 seconds (varies by configuration)
- **Cargo Space**: Approximately 69.6 cubic feet
- **Notable Features**: Advanced driver-assistance systems, modern infotainment with large touchscreen, multiple drive modes

## Recommendation
Both vehicles offer compelling features suited for a sales fleet. The Tesla Model Y edges ahead in range and charging infrastructure that may minimize downtime. However, if diverse pricing and trim options are essential, and a strong performance variant is appealing, then the Ford Mustang Mach-E could also be a great candidate. Ultimately, if range and efficient charging are the top priorities, the **Tesla Model Y** is recommended.
Both vehicles present compelling options for a sales fleet. The **Tesla Model Y** is exceptional in range and charging speed, ideal for longer trips without frequent stops. The **Ford Mustang Mach-E**, on the other hand, offers strong range options and flexible configurations, with a focus on performance and tech features.

**Recommendation**: Consider acquiring a mix of both vehicles. Tesla Model Y for its superior range and charging efficiency, complemented by the Ford Mustang Mach-E for varied performance characteristics and comfort.
